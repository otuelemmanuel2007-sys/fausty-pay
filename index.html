How to Pay

  • 1

    Open MoMo App

    Dial *170# or tap the button to launch MTN MoMo.

  • 2

    Select Send Money

    Choose "Transfer Money" then "Send Money" from the menu.

  • 3

    Enter Details

    Enter the number 0257704506 and the amount.

  • 4

    Confirm & Pay

    Review the details and enter your MoMo PIN to complete.
